Our Story

Founded on trust,
proven by experience.

Vukazizwe Cleaning Group was founded by Bongisizwe Nozaza, driven by a passion for creating clean, healthy, and welcoming environments while delivering professional cleaning services people can trust.

Before establishing Vukazizwe, Bongisizwe invested in professional training and earned a Certificate of Competence in Hygiene and Cleaning (Level 01) through Services SETA, providing a solid foundation in professional cleaning practices and hygiene standards.

The Tsebo Foundation

To further develop his expertise, Bongisizwe spent three years with Tsebo Cleaning and Hygiene Solutions, gaining valuable experience across three different client sites:

SITE A

EY (Ernst & Young) Offices

Maintaining commercial cleaning standards at EY offices.

SITE B

Private Hospital Placement

Delivering professional cleaning services in a private hospital, where cleanliness and infection prevention were essential.

SITE C

Gautrain Network

Providing hygiene and cleaning solutions at Gautrain, ensuring world-class cleanliness and hygiene in a high-traffic public transport environment.

SERVICE ETHOS

"These experiences equipped him with the skills, discipline, and attention to detail required to serve clients across residential, commercial, healthcare, and public facilities."

Currently Offering:

Today, Vukazizwe Cleaning Group proudly offers residential cleaning, deep cleaning, spring cleaning, oven cleaning, laundry services, and after-party or event cleaning. Every service is delivered with professionalism, integrity, and a commitment to exceeding our clients' expectations.

At Vukazizwe Cleaning Group, we believe that every clean space creates comfort, confidence, and peace of mind.
