Our Story

Vukazizwe Cleaning Group was founded by Bongisizwe Nozaza, driven by a passion for creating clean, healthy, and welcoming environments while delivering professional cleaning services people can trust.

Before establishing Vukazizwe, Bongisizwe invested in professional training and earned a Certificate of Competence in Hygiene and Cleaning (Level 01) through Services SETA, providing a solid foundation in professional cleaning practices and hygiene standards.

Foundational Expertise & Experience

To further develop his expertise, Bongisizwe spent three years with Tsebo Cleaning and Hygiene Solutions, gaining valuable experience across three different client sites:

Client Site Experience & Standards Delivered
EY (Ernst & Young) Maintaining high-level corporate cleaning standards at EY offices, ensuring prestige work spaces remained immaculate.
Private Hospital Delivering meticulous cleaning services in a private hospital setting, where clinical disinfection and infection prevention protocols were strictly essential.
Gautrain Providing rapid, robust hygiene and cleaning solutions, ensuring world-class cleanliness and safety inside high-traffic public transport environments.

These experiences equipped him with the unique set of skills, strict discipline, and fine attention to detail required to reliably serve clients across residential, commercial, healthcare, and public facilities.
